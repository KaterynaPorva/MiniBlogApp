Handles GET request to display the user's posts.

Handles POST request to delete a specific post.

Parameters
idThe unique identifier of the post to delete.

Verifies that the post belongs to the logged-in user before deleting it. If the post does not exist or belongs to another user, no action is taken.

IActionResult Redirects to the same page after deletion.
